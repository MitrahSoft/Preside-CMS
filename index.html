PresideCMS combines the power and flexibility of an enterprise CMS platform with the rapid delivery of the best web development frameworks.

About

Over 10 years of CMS experience

PresideCMS powers 100s of sites including large publishers and membership organisations. Engineered by Pixl8 Interactive, PresideCMS was originally launched in 2002 and is now in its 10th edition which has been re-engineered from the ground up and released as an open source project.

Getting started

To take PresideCMS for a spin, we recommend getting setup using our CommandBox commands. These allow you to scaffold a blank new site and spin up a pre-configured server in minutes. Full instructions can be found at the PresideCMS CommandBox commands repository.

Downloads

We highly recommend using the CommandBox approach to get started with PresideCMS. However, direct downloads can be found at our downloads repository, http://downloads.presidecms.com.

We're currently in a working beta and have both bleeding edge and stable beta releases available. There are no official release builds as yet. The latest builds can be found at:

  • Bleeding edge: PresideCMS-0.1.2.zip
  • Stable: PresideCMS-0.1.2.zip
